Questions

  1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags? Block tags create boxes and inline tags are used to modify text within a line.
  2. What is the margin, border, and padding? Margin is the space outside the border, border is the line around the element, and padding is the space inside the border.
  3. What are units of measurement you can use in CSS? There's fixed units like px, cm, and in, then there's relative units like %, em, and rem.
  4. When would you use a figure tag? Use it to make pictures in a block tag.
