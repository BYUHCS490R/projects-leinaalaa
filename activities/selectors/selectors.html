Questions:

  1. List out the different ways to select an item. Your could use an id, class, descendent, or a tag name.
  2. What is the difference between an ID and a Class? You use a period instead of a hashtag.
  3. What is the difference between a SPAN tag and a DIV tag? A DIV tag will cause line breaks and a SPAN tag does not cause line breaks.
  4. What is the difference between a DIV tag and a HEADER, MAIN, or FOOTER? They all act exactly the same way.
  5. What are the different ways to select a color? The different ways are by name, RGB ranging from 0-255, hex code, short hex code.
