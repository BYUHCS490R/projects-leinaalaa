Structural Tags

What goes in the header? The name of the website, the logo, navigation.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website? Links go in the navigation and it doesn't have to be in the header. Normally you wouldn't want a navigation link on your website that leads to another website because it's about your page.

What goes in Main? All content that is unique to that page. Main thing this webpage is about.

What goes in the Footer? Social media links, copyright info, contact info, legal info.
