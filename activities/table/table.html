Questions

  1. When do you use a table? When we have data we want to present in a structured format.
  2. When do you not use a table? When the data is not structured or when a simple list is more appropriate.
  3. When do you need to do extra work for table accessibility? When you want to ensure that the table is usable by people with disabilities, like people who use screen readers.
