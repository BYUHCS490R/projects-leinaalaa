About Us

Pono Tree Service LLC is a small family-owned business on the island of Kaua'i.

Jacob Akaka is one of the owners of the business and does all tree trimming with over 30 years of experience. As business partners, Jacob and Raquel Akaka obtained the necessary insurance licenses and certificates to legally operate a tree service business in Hawaii.
