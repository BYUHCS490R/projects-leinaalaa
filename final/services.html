Our Services

At Pono Tree Service LLC, we provide a range of tree care services to help maintain the health and beauty of your trees. Regular maintenance is important to prevent damage and keep trees strong. According to the Arbor Day Foundation, proper pruning improves tree structure and helps reduce the risk of falling branches.

Tree Care Services

  • Tree Trimming - Keeps trees healthy and safe
  • Tree Removal - Safely removes trees that are diseased, damaged, or pose a risk
  • Tree Inspection - Evaluates tree health and identifies potential issues
  • Tree Maintenance - Regular care to promote tree health.
  • Coconut Rhinoceros Beetle (CRB) Treatment - Specialized treatment for CRB infestations

Why These Services Matter

Tree care is important for both safety and environment health. Overgrown trees can become dangerous, especially during storms. In Hawai'i, invasive pests such as the coconut rhinoceros beetle can also damage trees and require proper treatment. The Hawaii Invasive Species Council explains how these pests affect palm trees and why early treatment is crucial.

Pono Tree Service LLC uses safe and proper practices and proper equipment to ensure every job is completed professionally.
